❔ About App

👥 Patients & Search
Add rooms with the ➕ Add Room button at the bottom. Tap a room name to expand/collapse it. Use the ⋮ button on rooms and patients for copy, reset, and delete options. Long-press the ⋮⋮ handle to drag patients between rooms.
❤️ Vitals & Trends
Tap any vital to open the smart numpad. Abnormal values automatically pulse red. Tap a vital to instantly view its Trend History.
✅ Task Manager
Create standard or timed tasks. Emergent tasks will trigger alerts. Swipe Left to delete, or Swipe Right to complete.
🛠️ Clinical Tools
The top toolbar gives quick access to Medscape AI, DDx Search, and Labs. You can customize this toolbar in Settings.
💾 Offline & Backups
MiriMate runs completely offline. Export your data as a backup file directly from the Settings menu.
